## Design context

Frontend design work is governed by two root files, written and maintained by the `impeccable` skill:

- `PRODUCT.md` — strategic design context: register (`product`), users, purpose, brand personality, anti-references, design principles, accessibility. Read it before any UI decision.
- `DESIGN.md` — the visual system: color strategy, typography direction, elevation, and component guidance (currently a `SEED`; re-run `/impeccable document` in scan mode once real components exist).

Before designing, building, or reviewing any interface, read both. Register is `product`; the public landing and invite-acceptance flow are the one `brand`-register exception.
